Operations Model for Facebook Teams

  1. Separate account clusters by objective and owner.
  2. Keep web admin and mobile actions in their optimal environments.
  3. Use repeatable handoff checklists across operators.
  4. Audit session consistency weekly before scaling.

Risk Checklist

  • No random profile switching without intent mapping.
  • No scaling before baseline stability is proven.
  • No undefined ownership for active workflow profiles.
  • No ad-hoc changes without documentation.
