Operations Model for Facebook Teams

  1. Separate account clusters by objective (ads, pages, support) and owner.
  2. Keep web admin and mobile actions in their optimal environments.
  3. Use repeatable handoff checklists across operators.
  4. Audit session consistency weekly before scaling profile count.
  5. Document verification events and recovery steps per profile cluster.

Risk Checklist

  • No random profile switching without intent mapping.
  • No scaling before baseline stability is proven over 7+ days.
  • No undefined ownership for active workflow profiles.
  • No ad-hoc permission or proxy changes without documentation.
  • No mixing unrelated business objectives in one profile lifecycle.

Scaling Model

Start with a small cohort (3–5 profiles), one operator, and one objective. When weekly QA shows stable sessions and clean handoffs, increase volume in batches. Pair this guide with billing cycle planning so spend tracks maturity.
