Multilogin Pricing: Monthly vs Annual

Use monthly for testing phases and annual for stable long-horizon operations.

Navigation

  • Decision framework
  • Cost model mindset
  • Related pages

How to Decide

  • Monthly: best for validation and quick iteration.
  • Annual: best for proven workflows and cost efficiency.
  • Measure stability, failure rate, and operator time cost.

Use coupons first: ADBNEW50 or SAVE50.

Cost Model Mindset

Focus on cost per stable output, not only monthly sticker price. If unstable workflows consume team time, a cheaper plan can become more expensive operationally.

Extended FAQ: Monthly vs Annual

Is annual always cheaper?
Nominally yes in many cases, but only if workflows are stable enough to benefit long term.
When should I switch to annual?
After process consistency, operator discipline, and failure trends are under control.
Do coupons still matter if I optimize process?
Yes. Coupons improve entry economics while process quality drives long-term ROI.
