## When Multilogin is often stronger

- You want **Browser + Cloud Phone** in one operational stack.
- Agencies need strict profile governance and documented SOP.
- Long-horizon campaigns punish unstable sessions.

Verified codes on this site: **SAAS50** (Browser) and **MIN50** (Cloud Phone). Apply steps: [promo code guide](/how-to-apply-multilogin-promo-code/).

## When GoLogin may be considered

- Solo operators with simple browser-only needs and tight upfront budget.
- Short pilots where mobile app workflows are out of scope.

Still run a 7-day pilot and measure cost per stable output, not sticker price.
