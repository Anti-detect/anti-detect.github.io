## Multilogin vs GoLogin
- **Multilogin:** Strong security, advanced features, higher price.
- **GoLogin:** More affordable, easy to use, suitable for individuals or small teams.

## When Should You Choose Multilogin?
- When you need maximum security and manage a large number of accounts.

## When Should You Choose GoLogin?
- When you have a limited budget and simple needs.

## FAQ
**Can you transfer data between the two platforms?**
Currently, there is no direct support; you need to do it manually.
